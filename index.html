Privacy Policy for VibeScout

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

VibeScout ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information when you create an account:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, username, and password
  • Profile Information: Profile picture, bio, and user preferences
  • Payment Information: Payment details processed securely through Stripe (we do not store credit card numbers)

1.2 Location Data

VibeScout requires precise location information to function properly:

  • Real-time Location: We collect your GPS coordinates to show nearby bounties and vibes on the map
  • Location History: We may store your location history to improve service and show relevant content
  • Purpose: Location data is essential for connecting scouts with local bounty opportunities

1.3 User-Generated Content

When you use VibeScout, you may create and share content:

  • Videos and Photos: Content you capture and submit for bounties
  • Messages: Chat messages between requesters and scouts
  • Reviews and Ratings: Feedback you provide about other users
  • Bounty Posts: Descriptions and details of bounties you create

1.4 Device Information

We automatically collect certain information about your device:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • Mobile network information
  • App version and usage statistics

1.5 Permissions We Request

  • Camera: To record videos and take photos for bounty deliveries
  • Microphone: To record audio with videos
  • Location: To show nearby bounties and enable location-based features
  • Storage: To save and access media files
  • Internet: To communicate with our servers and other users

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide and maintain the VibeScout platform
  • Matching: To connect scouts with relevant bounty opportunities based on location and preferences
  • Payments: To process payments for completed bounties through Stripe
  • Communication: To send notifications about bounty updates, matches, and messages
  • Safety: To monitor for fraudulent activity and ensure user safety
  • Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our services
  • Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

3. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

3.1 With Other Users

  • Your profile information (name, photo, ratings) is visible to other users
  • Your location is shown approximately to nearby scouts when you post a bounty
  • Content you submit for bounties is shared with the requester

3.2 Service Providers

  • Stripe: For payment processing
  • Supabase: For database and authentication services
  • Cloud Storage: For storing videos and media files
  • Analytics Providers: For app performance and usage analytics

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests from authorities.

3.4 Business Transfers

If VibeScout is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Content: Videos and photos are retained until you delete them or close your account
  • Transaction History: Payment records are retained for 7 years for legal and tax purposes
  • Location History: Retained for up to 90 days unless you request deletion

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct your information through your profile settings
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out: Disable location services or notifications in your device settings
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format

5.2 Account Deletion

To delete your account:

  1. Go to Settings in the app
  2. Select "Account Settings"
  3. Choose "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your decision

Note: Some information may be retained for legal compliance even after account deletion.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS
  • Secure Storage: Data at rest is encrypted in our secure databases
  • Authentication: Password-protected accounts with secure authentication
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access to personal data
  • Regular Audits: Security practices are regularly reviewed and updated

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

VibeScout is not intended for users under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with third-party services:

  • Google Maps: For displaying maps and location services (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Stripe: For payment processing (subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy)
  • Google Sign-In: For optional authentication (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these third-party services.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Sending a notification through the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

Your continued use of VibeScout after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Right to deletion of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

12. GDPR Rights (European Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent
